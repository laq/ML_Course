Error promedio

$E=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}{|y_i-y_i'|}$

Mean Absolute Error: MAE

$E=\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}{y_i^2 - y_i'^2}$

Mean Square Error: MSE

$E=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}{y_i^2 - y_i'^2}}$

Root Mean Square Error: RMSE
